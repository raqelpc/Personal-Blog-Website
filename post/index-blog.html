Why should we meditate?

Is it really worth the effort to learn something new that we are not sure will help us?

I have been interested for some time in the benefits of meditation.

Many scientific studies on the various types of meditation prove that there are several reasons why we should meditate. It is no accident that millions of people have meditated for thousands of years.

Meditation changes the mind and body in various ways, so there are numerous positive reasons why we should do so.

There really are several studies. However, one of the most rigorous studies done was about the practice of Mindfullness. This practice showed surprising results such as lower levels in their blood of a marker of unhealthy inflammation than the relaxation group, even though few were still meditating.

Remarkable reasons why we should practicing Mindfullness

  • Brain Health
  • Influencing addictions
  • Lowering blood pressure
  • Pain reduction
  • Quality sleep
  • Attention
  • Reduction of depression and anxiety
  • Stress reduction
